Frequently Asked Questions

What cryptocurrencies do you support?
We currently support these selected tokens: Bitcoin (BTC), Monero (XMR), Ethereum (ETH), USDT (TRC-20), and Solana (SOL). We focus on providing the most secure escrow services for these popular cryptocurrencies.
How do I start a trade?
Simply open our Telegram bot (@Vaultpayofficialbot), click "Create Escrow", select your trade type, and follow the instructions. The bot will guide you through every step.
What are your fees?
We charge a 1% service fee for P2P/OTC transactions and 0.5% per side (1% total) for coin swaps. These fees cover our escrow service only. Network transaction fees and processing fees are separate and paid directly to the blockchain network.
How secure is VaultPay?
We use secure group technology, transaction verification, and admin oversight for every trade. Funds are protected until both parties are satisfied with the transaction.
What happens if there's a dispute?
In case of disputes, our admin team reviews all chat history and transaction evidence within the secure group. We make fair decisions based on the available information to ensure both parties are treated fairly.
Where can I find trading partners?
Join our VaultPay Trading Hub Telegram group to connect with other traders, find trading partners, and get community support.
