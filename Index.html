Frequently Asked Questions

What is escrow?
Escrow is a secure payment method where funds are held by a trusted third party until both trading parties fulfill their obligations. This protects buyers and sellers from fraud.
How do I start a trade?
Simply open our Telegram bot (@Vaultpayofficialbot), click "Create Escrow", select your trade type, and follow the instructions. The bot will guide you through every step.
What cryptocurrencies do you support?
We support Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), USDT (TRC20), Solana (SOL), Monero (XMR), and many other popular cryptocurrencies for escrow services.
What are your fees?
We charge a 1% service fee for P2P/OTC transactions and 0.5% per side (1% total) for coin swaps. This ensures we can provide secure, reliable escrow services.
How secure is VaultPay?
We use secure group technology, transaction verification, and admin oversight for every trade. Funds are protected until both parties are satisfied with the transaction.
